# Executive Summary: Attrition Predictor

### **The Challenge: Stemming the Talent Leak**
LuminaTech Industries is facing a critical turnover rate of **33%** (1 in 3 employees). In today's "war for talent," this trend leads to massive replacement costs and a strategic loss of institutional knowledge. With an HR team of only 8 managers for 311 employees, LuminaTech lacks the tools to anticipate resignations proactively.

### **The Solution: Actionable & Responsible AI**
We have developed the **Employee Attrition Predictor v2.0**, an Artificial Intelligence solution designed not as a replacement for human judgment, but as a **decision-support tool**.
* **Performance:** Powered by a *Random Forest* algorithm, the model achieves a **0.88 ROC-AUC score**, ensuring high reliability in detecting at-risk profiles.
* **Transparency:** Every risk score is paired with its **top 3 explanatory factors (SHAP)**, allowing HR to understand *why* an employee might leave (e.g., lack of special projects, salary issues, or high absenteeism).

### **The 4 Pillars of Trusted AI**
1.  **Ethics & Legality:** The model is **"Privacy by Design."** All discriminatory variables (Sex, Race, Marital Status) were excluded from training in compliance with **GDPR** and **French Labor Law**. A fairness audit confirmed the absence of algorithmic bias.
2.  **Frugality:** Following Green IT principles, the AI is ultra-lightweight (< 5 MB), and its training consumes less energy than a single smartphone charge.
3.  **Cybersecurity:** The pipeline includes robustness tests against adversarial data injection and strict environment variable management for sensitive data.
4.  **EU AI Act Compliance:** Classified as a **"High-Risk"** system, it meets European requirements for transparency, human oversight, and traceability.

### **Expected Business Impact**
* **Reduced Turnover:** Transitioning from reactive management to targeted prevention.
* **HR Optimization:** Major time savings, allowing HR teams to focus on high-value retention interviews.
* **Brand Equity:** Strengthening LuminaTech’s reputation as an ethical and innovative employer.

### **Conclusion**
This project demonstrates that **predictive performance** and **radical ethics** are not mutually exclusive. LuminaTech now possesses a sustainable technological lever to stabilize its workforce and secure its future growth.
